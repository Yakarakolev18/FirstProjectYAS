ABOUT

The Blue Economy Initiative in Canada is a project funded by the Royal Bank of Canada, the Canadian Water Network, and the Walter and Duncan Gordon Foundation. It helps Canada redirect funding andresources into its water managing programmes.

MISSION

Canada's Blue Economy want to improve management decisions, policies and practices that ensure maximum social, environmental, and economic benefits to current and future generations.

GOALS

The three main goals of Canada's Blue Economy is to *Strengthen the national understanding of the

VALUE

*Illuminate the opportunities, BENEFITS and means for sustainable water management.

*Create broader awareness of the RISKS of failing to make sustainable decisions about water management by not accounting for its many values.
